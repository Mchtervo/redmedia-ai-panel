# CRM Engine

# Redmedia AI Growth OS

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

CRM Engine is the central customer management system of Redmedia AI.

Unlike traditional CRMs, this engine continuously learns from customer behavior, analyzes interactions, predicts future actions and automatically assists the sales team.

Every customer interaction becomes business intelligence.

---

# Objectives

Build a complete customer profile.

Track every interaction.

Increase conversion rate.

Improve customer satisfaction.

Reduce lost opportunities.

Support AI decision making.

Provide 360° customer visibility.
