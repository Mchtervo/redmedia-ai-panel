# Memory Engine

# Redmedia AI Growth OS

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

Memory Engine gives the AI a persistent business memory.

Unlike traditional chatbots, the AI should remember valuable business knowledge across days, weeks, months and years.

Every interaction should improve future decisions.

Memory is one of the most valuable assets of the platform.

# Forgetting Policy

The AI should never forget important business knowledge.

Only temporary context may expire.

Low-value temporary memories may be removed.

Business-critical knowledge is permanent unless manually deleted.
