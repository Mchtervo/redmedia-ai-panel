# Reservation Engine

# Redmedia AI Growth OS

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Reservation Engine manages the complete reservation lifecycle from the first customer inquiry to final delivery.

It coordinates customers, employees, schedules, payments, contracts, reminders and AI recommendations.

The goal is to eliminate manual reservation management and reduce human error.

---

# Objectives

- Centralize all reservations.
- Prevent scheduling conflicts.
- Automate reminders.
- Increase reservation completion.
- Improve customer experience.
- Reduce missed appointments.
- Support AI scheduling decisions.
