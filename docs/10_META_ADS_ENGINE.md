# Redmedia AI Growth OS

# Purpose

Meta Ads Engine is responsible for managing, synchronizing, analyzing and optimizing Facebook and Instagram advertising.

Unlike traditional dashboards, this module is capable of understanding campaign performance and continuously improving advertising strategy through AI.

Every campaign becomes a learning opportunity.
